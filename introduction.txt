Brief Introduction About Myself
Biniyam is a committed environmental science graduate student and researcher at Addis Ababa University, with a strong foundation in chemistry and a keen focus on sustainability and innovative waste management solutions. Over the course of his academic journey, Biniyam has developed expertise in energy, climate finance, and the circular economy, notably conducting research on the valorization of organic wastes, including the use of Black Soldier Fly (BSF) for resource recovery and environmental remediation.

In addition to his research, Biniyam is actively involved in teaching, offering one-on-one support to students in Maths and Science, and is recognized for fostering positive, holistic learning environments. His involvement in youth leadership initiatives, commitment to volunteerism, and adept use of digital tools for project management and communication highlight his broad skillset and dedication to making a tangible impact in the fields of sustainability and education.
